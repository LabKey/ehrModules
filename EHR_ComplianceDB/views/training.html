• This page provides a mechanism for WNPRC Trainers to directly enter the dates that staff complete training requirements.

  • Below is a grid that displays records of when each employee completed Complaince/Training requirements.

  • You can import new information using the 'Insert New' button. Employees must be entered using their employee ID. This is usually the beginning of their WNPRC email.

  • At the bottom of this page there is a list of all WNPRC employees, including their employee ID.

  • All grids can be sorted and filtered by clicking on the column header.
